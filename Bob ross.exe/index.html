THE Joy of Painting:
 The (unofficial) Computer application.
Paint along with Bob Ross in a whole new way!





Instructions 'n' Tips

  • Use the mouse to draw.
  • Click the delete key to clear the canvas or press the "X" button at
    the bottom of the canvas.
  • If you wish to keep your painting, press the "<" at the
    bottom of the canvas to save a png of your painting to your computer, i'm not installing a virus to your computer, I promise.
  • Use the arrow keys to change the brush size.
  • Mixing colours is no bueno so click the rectangle below the preset
    colours to best match the colour being used.
  • Use a smaller brush size and click instead of dragging for certain
    parts of the painting to make it look better.
  • You can pause the video if you fall behind.
  • Make sure to prep the canvas before you begin to match the
    colour of the video.
  • You can save your work as a PNG by clicking the "save" button
    at the bottom of the canvas.
  • TAKE YOUR TIME.
  • Make happy little trees and have fun.
